304 Center Cap

3D printed center cap inspired by Method Race Wheels. Precision fit and clean styling. CAD-modeled and tested for secure hub engagement. This will be offered as a free STL or optionally printed in-house and shipped. Designed to offer an inexpensive alternative to replacing your center cap.

Status Update

This 304-style center cap is currently being optimized to fit a variety of hub bore diameters beyond the original 108mm Method sizing. The goal is to make the design flexible and accessible for more vehicle/wheel setups without sacrificing secure fitment.

The outer shell geometry is complete, and I’m currently testing multiple versions to verify snap-in retention, depth, and tolerance when printed in different filaments like PETG and ASA. I’m also refining the inner groove that accepts a compression-fit o-ring for extra grip.

This part will be offered as a purchaseable STL download via Cults3d, or optionally printed and shipped for those without 3D printers. If you’re interested in a specific bore size, feel free to reach out and I may be able to size it for your setup.

The Method logo shown in some preview images is for reference only and will likely be excluded from the shared STL due to trademark concerns.

Estimated availability for final STL and/or first batch of prints is **around 6 weeks** depending on final test prints and demand.
