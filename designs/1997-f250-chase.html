1997 F-250 Chase Light

Status: Early stages of testing. ~ 2 month ETA

This chase light is being designed as a third brake light replacement for the 1997 Ford F-250 with the 7.3L Power Stroke diesel. The design is currently in prototype phase and awaiting test-fitment due to limited access to the vehicle.

Goals include precise OEM-like fitment, weather-resistant ASA construction, and a clean install experience with optional amber pod integration. Development is ongoing and will be updated after test-fit data is collected.
