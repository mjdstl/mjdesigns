MJD Chase Light – 10th Gen F-150 (1997–2003)

Desert-tested chase light system with configurable LED pods and integrated brake/running lights.

This CAD-designed chase light replaces your stock third brake light, offering chase functionality while maintaining an OEM-style fit. The housing is 3D printed in ASA for heat and UV resistance, and mounts in the factory third-brake-light location on 1997–2004 Ford F-150s. A wiring diagram and install notes are provided. Basic wiring and modifications are required on installation.

What’s Included

  • 1x 3D-printed ASA chase light housing, in black.
  • 2x 40 W LED pods in the lens/beam option you select below.
  • 2x Compact LEDs for brake lights and 1x compact LED for running lights, all mounted inside the housing.
  • 1x Pre installed foam gasket material to seal the housing to the cab.

What You’ll Need to Supply

  • 12V power, ground, and tap into your third-brake and running light circuits.
  • Switches, fuse, and relay for the chase pods (recommended on their own fused circuit).
  • Optional: strobe controller module if you want chase/strobe patterns on the amber pods.

Recommended Electrical Setup

I recommend running the chase pods on their own dedicated 12 V wiring harness with an in-line fuse, switch and strobe controller. On my own truck, the amber chase pods are connect to a switch panel with a built in fuse/relay and an additional strobe controller module to wirlessly enable chase patterns. The brake and running lights are connected by tapping into the factory third-brake and running light circuits. More info coming soon on wiring diagrams and setup suggestions.

Choose Your Lens Configuration

All options use the same 3" 40 W LED pods — you’re choosing the lens and beam pattern. I currently run Yellow Amber Diffused on my own truck (shown above). Yellow Amber Combo is the default desert setup if you don’t pick a specific option. Diffused honeycomb lenses are more about being seen than seeing the trail — they’re designed for maximum presence, not maximum distance.
