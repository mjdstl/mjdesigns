10th Gen F-150 Chase Light

This CAD-designed chase light replaces your stock third brake light offering chase light functionality while maintaining OEM fit and style. Made from ASA filament for heat and UV resistance. Designed to fit 1997–2004 Ford F-150, with wiring diagram included. Modifications required upon installation.

Status Update

The latest build of the 10th gen F-150 Chase Light has been installed on my truck for a while now, and I’ve been purposely holding off on posting anything until I could see how it handled real weather. So far, it has passed every test — heat, moisture, and heavy rain — with zero issues. The amber pods, brake lights, and running light are all still working perfectly, and I haven’t seen any signs of water intrusion.

This version fits flush to the cab, reuses the OEM third-brake-light screws, and the foam gasket compresses cleanly to make a solid seal. Tomorrow, I’m doing a full teardown to confirm everything internally before finalizing anything or moving forward with sales.

I’ve also noticed a couple of other 10th-gen chase light designs popping up lately, which is cool to see — but it also makes me glad I’ve taken the time to make sure mine is durable, watertight, and designed with more features. I’d rather release something that works 1000% correctly than rush something out.

Install notes remain the same: the amber chase pods require their own 12 V switch or wiring harness, and the third-brake-light wiring taps into the OEM harness. A wiring diagram and optional harness & strobe module recommendations are coming soon.

More updates after tomorrow’s inspection.
