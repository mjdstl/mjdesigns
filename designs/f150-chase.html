10th Gen F-150 Chase Light

This CAD-designed chase light replaces your stock third brake light offering chase light functionality while still keeping OEM fit and style. Made from ASA filament for heat and UV resistance. Designed to fit 1997–2004 Ford F-150, with wiring diagram included. Modifications required upon installation.

Status Update

The 10th Gen F-150 Chase Light is now in its final phase. The latest ASA flagship version fits my own truck perfectly — it reuses the OEM third brake light screws and mounts flush with the cab. The shape, contour, and alignment with the upper trim are now dialed in.

I’ve been testing this version daily to confirm durability, seal integrity, and light performance. The amber pods are fully integrated with my strobe controller and switch panel, and both the running and brake lights use the newer compact 11 mm modules for a cleaner look and stronger output.

Installation will still require connecting the new brake and running light leads into the OEM harness, plus a separate 12 V feed for the amber chase pods. I’ll post wiring diagrams and optional harness suggestions soon to make setup easier for anyone planning their own install.

Thank you to everyone who’s followed along and given feedback — it’s made a huge difference getting this design to where it’s at now. This is the cleanest, most refined version yet, and I’m excited to finally get it into more 10th-gen F-150 builds soon.
