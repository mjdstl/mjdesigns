10th Gen F-150 Chase Light

This CAD-designed chase light replaces your stock third brake light offering chase light functionality while maintaining OEM fit and style. Made from ASA filament for heat and UV resistance. Designed to fit 1997–2004 Ford F-150, with wiring diagram included. Modifications required upon installation.

Status Update

I finally got the chance to take my truck out to Ocotillo Wells during Thanksgiving, and it was the first real test of the chase light in busy, dusty conditions. There were a lot of people out there, tons of lights everywhere, and it can be easy to lose track of vehicles at night or even during the day in the dust.

I drove across the desert from Pole Line down past Blow Sand while riding with a group of dirt bikers, and they said the chase light was super helpful. The amber pods stayed bright and visible in the dust, and the brake lights were easy to see the entire time. From my own experience out there, it was great having the chase light on at night with so much traffic around — nobody came up behind me too fast, everyone could see me clearly, and it made pulling into camp and unloading the trailer way easier.

This version has now been through heat, moisture, rain, dust, vibration, and constant movement, and it’s still performing exactly how I designed it to. The last thing I want to do before moving forward is fully dissect the housing and inspect everything internally to confirm the seal and overall durability. That will be happening very soon.

Once that final inspection is done, I’ll be doing a fully revamped update of this page to get the chase light ready for purchase. I’ll also be posting on social media once everything is confirmed, and I plan on showing part of the teardown process so people can see how well the design protected itself from the conditions.

More updates after tomorrow’s inspection.
