About MJDesigns

MJDesigns is an independent design studio specializing in custom off-road lighting, 3D-printed automotive components, and functional engineering solutions. What began as a single-person prototyping effort has grown into a small, focused team dedicated to building clean, reliable, and OEM-inspired products.

Every MJDesigns product is created through a complete process — from CAD modeling and prototype iteration to real-world testing across Southern California’s deserts, trails, and daily-driving conditions. Each design is developed with durability, fitment, and user experience at the core.

Our goal is simple: produce dependable, well-designed components that look great, install easily, and improve capability where it matters. Whether you're exploring new terrain or upgrading your build, MJDesigns is committed to delivering parts that perform.

Thank you for supporting and following the growth of MJDesigns. More designs, upgrades, and refinements are on the way.
