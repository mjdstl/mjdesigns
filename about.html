About MJDesigns

MJDesigns is a one-person design studio led by Mike Jennings, focused on creating clean, practical, and customizable 3D-printed solutions for automotive and lifestyle applications.

Every design featured here is developed from scratch, tested, and fine-tuned in Southern California using high-quality materials and real-world fitment trials.

This site isn't just about selling — it’s a showcase of ideas in motion. Each product is a work in progress, shaped by hands-on experience and community feedback. Thanks for stopping by and supporting.
